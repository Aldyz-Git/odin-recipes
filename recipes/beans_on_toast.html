Bean's On Toast

A quick easy delicious recipe for people on the go!

Description

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tin of heinz baked beans
  • 40g of grated mature cheddar

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place both slices of white bread in toaster.

  2. Step 2

    3. Warm beans on the hob for 3 - 4 mins stirring constantly.

  3. Step 3

    4. Grate cheese.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place toast on plate. Pour on beans.

  5. Step 5

    6. Sprinkle with cheese according to taste.
